# Refresh Policy

Use this when the copy is older, published, or tied to changed product reality.

## Refresh when
- the core audience and angle still hold
- stats, examples, screenshots, pricing, or proof are stale
- the structure basically works but facts or language drifted

## Rewrite when
- the audience changed
- the offer or category framing changed
- the page no longer matches channel intent
- the current structure cannot support the new story honestly

## Refresh checks
- update dates, metrics, screenshots, plans, and feature claims
- verify every link, proof point, and quoted customer result
- remove dead trends, old launches, and expired comparisons
- add fresher proof if it exists
- keep a visible list of unresolved proof gaps instead of faking certainty
