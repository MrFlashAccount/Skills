# Sales-enablement contract

Build a short contract before drafting collateral.

## Required fields

- `Asset`: deck, one-pager, champion note, proposal, objection doc, demo narrative, FAQ, or multi-asset pack
- `Audience`: rep, champion, evaluator, economic buyer, technical buyer, or internal stakeholder
- `Deal stage`: prospecting, discovery, evaluation, pilot, procurement, expansion, renewal, or post-launch follow-up
- `Goal`: what this asset should help the reader do next
- `Message spine`: problem, differentiator, proof, and ask
- `Proof available`: customer stories, product facts, screenshots, implementation facts, pricing notes, or explicit constraints
- `Top objections`: price, risk, timing, switching, integration, security, or fit concerns
- `Dependencies`: pricing decision, launch brief, docs, security answers, case studies, or product screenshots
- `Risk points`: fake ROI, generic collateral, too much detail, weak proof, or stage mismatch

## Compact template

- Asset / Audience / Stage
- Goal / Message spine
- Proof / Constraints
- Objections
- Dependencies / Risks / Unknowns

## Critique prompts

- Could a rep find the answer they need in under ten seconds?
- Is the asset trying to do too many jobs at once?
- Which claim would a skeptical buyer challenge first?
- What does the champion need to forward internally after the call?
- What depends on pricing, docs, or product proof that is not ready yet?
