# Copywriting Task Contract

Lock these before drafting:

- artifact and surface: homepage, landing page, pricing page, ad, email block, one-pager, etc.
- target audience: ICP, persona, or buyer segment in scope
- awareness stage: unaware, problem-aware, solution-aware, product-aware, decision-ready
- channel and traffic context: organic, paid search, paid social, outbound, lifecycle, partner, sales follow-up
- primary conversion action: demo, signup, trial, reply, download, buy, contact
- product truth and capability boundaries
- proof inventory: metrics, testimonials, logos, case studies, examples, constraints
- main objection or friction to resolve
- brand/tone constraints and legal/compliance boundaries
- open questions that still limit claim strength

If anything above is missing, recover it from source material or shared GTM context before asking. Ask only for the blocker that materially changes the copy.
