# Output Formats

Default editing package:

## Revised copy
- final cleaned copy in full
- keep section labels if the source surface uses them

## Change notes
- what changed materially
- why the edit improves clarity, proof, audience fit, or conversion flow

## Risk notes
- unsupported claims that were softened
- proof still missing
- places where a full rewrite may be better than further editing

Optional when useful:
- before/after table for high-stakes lines
- refresh checklist for published assets
