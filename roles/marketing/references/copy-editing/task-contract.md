# Copy-Editing Task Contract

Capture these before editing:

- artifact and current state: draft, published page, campaign asset, email, deck, etc.
- what must stay the same: audience, offer, CTA, tone, structure, legal language, proof blocks, or nothing
- what feels wrong now: clarity, tone drift, weak proof, outdated facts, friction, verbosity, stale positioning
- source of truth for accuracy: current site, product notes, GTM context, approved proof, brand constraints
- whether the asset is live and whether factual drift is likely
- desired deliverable: cleaned copy only, annotated edits, or keep/change table

Ask only for the minimum blocker. If the copy is live or old, explicitly decide whether this is a refresh or a rewrite.
