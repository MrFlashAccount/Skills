# Deliverable Shapes

## Research synthesis
- decision being informed
- top themes
- segment differences
- strongest evidence
- implications for positioning / copy / launch / sales
- open questions

## VOC quote bank
- organized by pain, trigger, outcome, objection, alternative, proof expectation
- source/date attached
- exact language preserved

## ICP / persona summary
- segment definition
- buying context
- JTBD
- pains and stakes
- objections
- alternatives
- channels / communities / trust sources
- explicit fit / non-fit

## JTBD map
- functional job
- emotional job
- social job
- trigger events
- success criteria
- current alternatives

## Objection + proof matrix
- objection
- why it appears
- proof needed to answer it
- available evidence now
- missing evidence

## Research-gap memo
Use when the evidence is too thin for strong positioning.
State what is missing, why it matters, and the cheapest next evidence to collect.
