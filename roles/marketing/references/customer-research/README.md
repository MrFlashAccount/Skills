# Customer Research Reference

Use for evidence gathering and synthesis that supports marketing decisions.

## Use when

- customer research, VOC mining, ICP work, persona building, JTBD analysis
- review mining, interview synthesis, support or sales-call analysis
- churn, win/loss, objection, and proof-point synthesis
- customer-language extraction for downstream messaging

## Do not use when

- the main job is competitor site dossiers from URLs
- the task is final copywriting, launch execution, or collateral production
- the output would rely on invented persona traits or fake certainty

## Workflow

1. Clarify the decision the research must improve.
2. Inventory available evidence before asking for more.
3. Separate analyzing existing evidence from gathering new evidence.
4. Tag findings by segment, source, date, and confidence.
5. Extract ICP/JTBD, pains, triggers, outcomes, objections, proof, alternatives, and exact language.
6. Synthesize patterns, contradictions, and gaps.
7. Deliver the smallest artifact that unblocks the decision.

## Deliverables

- research synthesis
- VOC quote bank
- ICP / persona summary
- JTBD map
- objection and proof-point matrix
- research-gap memo

## Rules

- Evidence before messaging.
- Keep segment boundaries explicit.
- Capture exact customer wording when it materially shapes claims or copy.
- Weight recent evidence above stale evidence.
- Separate frequency from intensity and label confidence.
- Keep product reality separate from customer wishes.
