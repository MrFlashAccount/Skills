# Content-Strategy Task Contract

Before planning, lock:

- business goal: pipeline, demand capture, education, launch support, expansion, retention, sales enablement, etc.
- target audience and buying context
- product truth and offer boundaries
- current channel scope: blog, email, social support, sales collateral, partner, launch page, multi-channel
- available evidence: search data, sales objections, support themes, research notes, interviews, win/loss, competitor gaps
- available resources: team, cadence, formats, review constraints
- success signal: traffic quality, demo intent, influenced pipeline, adoption, replies, backlinks, etc.
- time horizon and campaign window
- open questions that block prioritization

If evidence is thin, say so and reduce confidence instead of pretending the roadmap is validated.
