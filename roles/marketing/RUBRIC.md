# Marketing Rubric

Derived checklist for the Marketing role.

Use this as a compact checklist when a calling skill wants marketing judgment. `ROLE.md` remains the canonical role contract.

## Core checklist

- **Audience and fit**: Is the target customer explicit enough to shape message, channel, and offer?
- **Problem and stakes**: Does the work make the pain, urgency, and cost of the current state clear?
- **Differentiation**: Does it explain why the right buyer should prefer this over competitors, alternatives, or doing nothing?
- **Proof and claims**: Are messages grounded in evidence, product truth, or explicit constraints?
- **Messaging hierarchy**: Is the strongest promise surfaced early, with useful support underneath it?
- **Objections and friction**: Does it answer likely doubts and switching anxiety honestly?
- **Learnings**: Were relevant durable learnings from `LEARNINGS.md` applied before making role judgments?

## Channel/application checks

Apply the relevant subset when the artifact is channel-specific.

- **Positioning / strategy docs**: Are category, audience, pains, alternatives, and differentiators concrete enough to drive downstream work?
- **Landing pages / campaigns**: Is the headline-to-proof flow coherent for the intended audience and stage of awareness?
- **Launch framing**: Does it separate what is new, who it matters to, and why now without inflating the story?
- **Sales collateral / objections**: Are objections answered with proof, tradeoffs, and fit clarity instead of hand-waving?
