# Marketing Site

Use for product marketing sites, launch pages, and storytelling-led surfaces.

## Defaults

- more air
- larger type and clearer section rhythm
- stronger storytelling arc
- fewer elements per viewport
- more deliberate visual pacing

## What good looks like

- strong first-screen payoff
- clear section-to-section narrative progression
- emphasis controlled through hierarchy, not clutter
- spacing that creates confidence and perceived quality

## Common failures

- trying to look premium through effects instead of structure
- too many mixed messages in the first screen
- overly dense sections that read like dashboards or docs
- component repetition with no pacing or narrative shape
